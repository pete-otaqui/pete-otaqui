What is this thing?

This is a password generator, taking cues from a comic by XKCD. It turns out that pass phrases are better than pass words in terms of both ease-of-remembering, and being hard-to-guess.

Use the controls below to set the structure of the generated phrases, including symbols and numbers if required (lots of pssword systems require users to include these) and also capitalisation rules.

In supported browsers you will be able to tap / click a pass phrase to copy it to the clipboard.
